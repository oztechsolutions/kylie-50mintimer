How to use Rhythm
App setup and workflow
  1. Open Settings to configure your Govee lamp or set custom stage durations and colors.
  2. Save settings to persist them locally for next use.
  3. Tap Start to begin the session timer.
  4. Use the Pause button while running to stop and resume the timer.
  5. When the session ends, the screen shows a completion page and auto-ready countdown.
Tip: The timer works without the lamp, and the lamp color is optional.
